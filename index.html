Explore the 8 Wonders of the World

Great Wall of China

One of the greatest architectural feats in history, built to protect Chinese states from invasions.

Petra, Jordan

An ancient city carved into rose-red cliffs, once a thriving trading hub.

Colosseum, Italy

A grand Roman amphitheater that hosted gladiator battles and public spectacles.

Machu Picchu, Peru

An ancient Incan city set high in the Andes, known for its sophisticated dry-stone walls.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

A large pre-Columbian city built by the Maya civilization, featuring the famous El Castillo pyramid.

Taj Mahal, India

A stunning white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan for his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Christ the Redeemer, Brazil

A towering statue of Jesus Christ overlooking Rio de Janeiro, symbolizing peace and love.

Angkor Wat, Cambodia

The largest religious monument in the world, originally built as a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu.
