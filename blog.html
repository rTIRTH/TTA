Great Wall of China

Built over centuries to protect Chinese states from invasions, it stretches over 13,000 miles.

Petra, Jordan

Carved into red sandstone cliffs, Petra was a thriving trade city of the Nabataean kingdom.

Colosseum, Italy

A grand Roman amphitheater that hosted gladiator battles, capable of holding 50,000 spectators.

Machu Picchu, Peru

An Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains, known for its advanced engineering.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

An important Mayan city, home to the famous pyramid El Castillo, used for astronomical purposes.

Taj Mahal, India

Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and beauty.

Christ the Redeemer, Brazil

This colossal statue of Jesus stands atop Mount Corcovado, overlooking Rio de Janeiro.

Angkor Wat, Cambodia

The largest religious monument in the world, originally built as a Hindu temple and later a Buddhist site.
